## Clinical Safety Rule

Never generate clinical advice without evidence backing:

1. All health claims must reference at least one EvidenceRecord
2. RED risk tier topics require Grade A evidence or human review
3. Never state diagnoses ("you have X") — use "symptoms may be associated with"
4. Never recommend specific drug dosages — defer to "as prescribed by your provider"
5. Emergency symptoms always include "call 911 / go to ER immediately"
6. Never discourage seeking professional medical care
7. Safety alerts from FDA cascade immediately to all affected content
